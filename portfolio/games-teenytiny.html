Teeny Tiny

Teeny Tiny is a 3D Competitive Scavenger Hunt game with a twist of hide-and-seek. As the Tiny (these small strange little creatures) tasked to source food for your village, you must brave the giant's room to find the bits and pieces of leftovers to survive. But that's not just all, with other villages at war with yours and the giant making his rounds, will you be the first to grab it all?

I worked on the scoring system, including item depositing at the designated areas and scoreboard. This project was done in a group of 6 members, as part of CS3247 Game Development Project.


    Objectives
  • Two items to be collected will be released each time for players to compete and fight for the items.
  • To win, each player needs to collect the items and drop it at a collection point.
  • Players may use the skills at their disposal to slow down their opponents.
  • Player that collects 3 items the fastest wins!
